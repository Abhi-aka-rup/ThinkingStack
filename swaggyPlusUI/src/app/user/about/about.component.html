It all started in 2020 when a team of potential engineers decided they wanted to make life easier by changing the way India eats - all with just a tap!



With the idea of ’hyperlocal food delivery’, all they needed was the tech to power it and their vision to life with website.
And with this, SwaggyPlus was launched as a food ordering & delivery platform.



In May of 2020, Swiggy began operations by signing
up a few restaurants in Kolkata,West Bengal.
Soon enough, the first team of Hunger Saviors came into action
to deliver food within 40 minutes.
The technology that delivers great food right to your
doorstep was completely developed in-house!



With love and support from consumers,
SwaggyPlus shall expanded far and wide,
first through the entire city of Kolkata
and then across the entire country.
As they say, every big thing starts with a small dream.
